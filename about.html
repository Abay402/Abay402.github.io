About Me

My name is Abayjit Sidhu and I am a game developer. Developing games started as a hobby for me when I was around 15 years old but has since turned into an actual career. I attended Humber College in Toronto for 3 years and graduated with honours in the Game Programming program. Since graduating I've spent time working on small projects and tools while I search for my first job within the industry.

As a junior game developer I am always learning new skills and ways to design code more effectively. I try to develop games with the mindset that innovation is extremely important in making a good game. I also believe that developers should have fun playing the games their making. In terms of coding, I believe that smart, clean, and efficient code are the keys to success.

I have been playing video games my entire life and I understand just how important games are to people. With every project I work on, I will always try to use my skills in different engines and multiple coding languages to create fun gameplay experiences for players.
